About me

I am Kevin Hoang Le, a software engineer/programmer/developer/coder. I don't claim to be an expert in any area, but I have always been result-oriented and I deliver. One reason that what makes me succesful is I am very passionate about what I am doing, which is programming/writing software. In my opinion, I have an aptitude for software development, but that also means I try very hard, and I am eager to learn. Another reason that makes me succesful is because I benefit from a great wealth of information sharing on the Internet nowaday. I have read many good blogs and used ideas from countless number of talented people. One last but not least character trait about myself is I am committed to never stop learning.

About this site

Because I benefit so much from many people who have shared, I would like to do the same thing and return the favors. This site provides the channel for me to share my thoughts, the code I have written in my own time, and the lessons that I have learned. I don't claim that they are always right, but I guarantee that they are always my own ideas and the code I release are written myself. Wherever applicable, I will mention the source of my inspirations and the code snippets I have copied from. In return, you are invited to give me feedbacks and spread the information about what you have seen here. Most of the code I release will be under some kind of Open Source licenses. Again, last but not least, I would like this site to be the proof that I never want to stop learning.
